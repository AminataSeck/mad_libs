What is uTsAB(B)?

uTsAB(B) is a phrasal template word game where one player prompts others for a list of words to substitute for blanks in a story,
before reading the – often comical or nonsensical – story aloud.
The game is frequently played as a party game or as a pastime.
Categories are based on the letters of the creators names that makes one of their amazing instructors name!
